8. Brand Governance

Who owns the brand?

The Marketing and Design team owns and maintains this document. Questions about brand usage should be directed to the brand lead.

When to update this document

This Brand Bible should be reviewed and updated:

- When a visual rebrand or logo update occurs
- When company positioning or messaging shifts significantly
- Annually, at minimum, to ensure accuracy

Using the brand externally

Partners, ecosystem projects, and media outlets wishing to use the Envio name or logo should refer to the official Press Kit at https://envio.dev or contact the team directly.
