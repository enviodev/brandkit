4. Visual Identity

Logo

- The Envio wordmark is the primary logo. It should always be rendered clearly and never distorted, rotated, or recolored outside of approved variations.
- Maintain adequate clear space around the logo: at minimum, the height of the letter E in the wordmark on all sides.
- Do not place the logo on busy backgrounds that reduce legibility.
- Do not add drop shadows, gradients, or outlines to the logo.

Iconography and Illustration Style

- Icons should be line-based, minimal, and consistent in stroke weight.
- Illustrations, when used, should lean into abstract, geometric, or tech-inspired visuals; circuit-like patterns, network graphs, and data flow diagrams align with the brand.
- Avoid stock-photo-style imagery that looks generic or overly corporate.
- Dark-mode first: all visual assets should be designed to look best on dark backgrounds.
