# Quick Reference

Use this page when you need fast decisions without reading every guide.

## Logo

- Default: `logos/envio-logo-primary.svg`
- Dark background: `logos/envio-logo-white.svg`
- Square placements: `logos/envio-logo-1x1-clear.svg` or `logos/envio-logo-1x1-black.svg`

## Spacing and Size

- Clear space: at least `1x` the height of the `E`
- Minimum digital size: `24px` height

## Color

- Envio Orange: `#FF8267`
- Envio Black: `#0F0F0F`
- Orange is accent, not primary fill

## Voice

- Keep copy clear and outcome-focused
- Avoid hype and jargon-heavy phrasing
- Write with confidence and specificity

## Before Publishing

- Check logo choice and contrast
- Check color consistency
- Check copy tone against messaging guide
- Confirm final asset export quality
