2. Brand Positioning

Positioning Statement

For Web3 developers and engineering teams who need to build data-rich blockchain applications, Envio is the blockchain data indexing platform that delivers unmatched speed and developer experience so teams can ship faster and scale confidently.

Unlike legacy indexing solutions that are slow to set up and difficult to maintain, Envio's HyperIndex is designed from the ground up for modern development workflows.

Target Audience

Primary

- Blockchain / Web3 developers
- dApp developers building on EVM chains
- Protocol engineers needing on-chain analytics
- Full-stack engineers entering Web3

Secondary

- CTOs and engineering leads at Web3 startups
- DeFi and NFT protocol teams
- Web3 data analysts
- Blockchain infrastructure investors

Competitive Differentiators

- Blazing fast sync speeds: HyperSync technology for near-instant historical data ingestion
- Multi-language support: Write indexers in JavaScript, TypeScript, or ReScript
- Multi-chain support: Index across multiple EVM-compatible networks simultaneously
- Superior DX: Minimal boilerplate, fast local development, and clear documentation
- Open source: Community-driven with full transparency
